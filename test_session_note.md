# Session 99 - The Dragon's Hoard

**Date**: 2026-03-15
**Party**: Level 12
**Location**: Scaldmaw Swamp

---

## Summary

The party traveled to the abandoned dwarven fortress of Ironhold, seeking the legendary Dragon's Hoard. Inside, they encountered several interesting characters and discovered ancient artifacts.

## NPCs Encountered

- **Grimtooth the Merchant** - A sneaky goblin merchant selling rare goods from a hidden stall in the dungeon. He deals in stolen goods and doesn't ask questions.
- **Mira Stonebrook** - A half-elf ranger who has been tracking the dragon for months. She seeks revenge for her village that was destroyed.
- **Thornwick** - The dungeon caretaker, an elderly dwarf who has tended to Ironhold for centuries. Claims to serve no master.

## Locations Discovered

- **The Rusty Anvil** - An ancient dwarven forge deep within the fortress, still glowing with magical heat.
- **The Whispering Gallery** - A long corridor where echoes carry strange messages. Many who enter report hearing voices of their past.
- **Dragon's Throne Room** - The final chamber, filled with gold and dominated by a massive obsidian throne.

## Objects Found

- **Crown of the Mountain King** - A golden crown encrusted with rubies, said to grant dominion over stone and earth.
- **Emberheart Blade** - A longsword that glows with inner fire, warm to the touch.
- **Map of the Underdark** - An ancient map showing tunnels beneath the fortress.

## Organizations

- **The Iron Guild** - A secret society of treasure hunters who have been trying to reach the hoard for generations. Their symbol is a golden key.
- **Cult of the Eternal Flame** - A group of fire worshippers who believe the dragon is their god incarnate.

## Important Decisions

The party chose to:
1. Pay Grimtooth for information about traps
2. Form an alliance with Mira against the dragon
3. Spare Thornwick and learn his secrets

## Looted Items

- 500 gold pieces
- 3 rubies (worth 100gp each)
- A mysterious silver key
